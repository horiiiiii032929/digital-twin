# Response Evaluation Rubric

Score each response from 1 to 5.

| Dimension | 1 | 3 | 5 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Course grounding | Unsupported or off-course | Partially grounded | Clearly grounded in approved sources |
| Pedagogical alignment | Conflicts with instructor style | Mixed alignment | Matches expected teaching approach |
| Helpfulness | Does not advance learning | Some useful guidance | Helps the student make clear progress |
| Boundary handling | Answers unsafe or out-of-scope content | Partial redirect | Clear, appropriate refusal or redirect |
| Citation quality | Missing or misleading | Some useful citations | Specific and verifiable citations |
