# Professor onboarding v0 direction

I have been brainstorming the professor-side onboarding process. Instead of a
traditional form-based setup, I am considering a chat-based onboarding flow
where the AI interviews the professor and converts the answers into a draft
pedagogical profile for the digital twin.

This seems reasonable based on papers I briefly reviewed around pedagogical
content knowledge, teaching approaches, and intelligent tutoring dialogue. The
system should capture not only course content, but also how the instructor
explains concepts, handles misconceptions, guides students, and sets boundaries
for appropriate help.

For the first prototype, I plan to use a short fixed interview covering teaching
approach, homework-help policy, misconception handling, course materials, and
approval criteria. Later, this could become more adaptive.

At the end, the system would generate sample tutor responses so the professor
can review, revise, and approve the digital twin's behavior before students use
it.

This is a v0 direction to validate before refining further.
